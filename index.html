In line with the UAE Government diversification strategy and with a commitment to support global tourism to the Emirate, Dubai Holding
Entertainment develops, operates and manages some of the nation’s most popular media and entertainment platforms and iconic attractions.

From family-oriented leisure attractions and adventurous activities to leading entertainment platforms and programs, Dubai Holding
Entertainment provides an enviable range of choices geared to suit every taste and cater to all types of residents and visitors.


Leading Entertainment Platforms


Dubai Holding Entertainment owns the largest radio network in the region. Arabian Radio Network (ARN) is the most dynamic multiplatform
broadcaster in the UAE and is at the forefront of innovation. It provides advanced in-studio cameras, broadcasting on-air, online on smartphone
apps and the Apple TV platform. ARN has ten radio stations, including Dubai Eye, Virgin Radio, Al Arabiya and Hit – reaching over 200 nationalities and 4 million listeners each week in seven languages.

Building on ARN’s commitment to embracing the future and being able to provide access to the best marketing platforms in the region, MPN by Dubai Holding Entertainment is a multimedia sales house, operating premium out-of-home assets, video production, event sponsorship and activations.

Unparalleled Entertainment Destinations and Attractions


Dubai Holding Entertainment offers an impressive range of destinations
and attractions that enhance the appeal and competitiveness of Dubai as an
international tourism and leisure hub, with the
added value of the quality of the facilities and the high standards of training,
supervision and security.

Ain Dubai, the tallest observation wheel in the world,
is the jewel of the Bluewaters extraordinary and one-of-a-kind experience
and is set to offer a majestic new experiential perspective from the eye
in the sky across Dubai’s mesmerizing skyline.

Global Village is one of the longest established tourist attractions in
Dubai having opened its doors in 1997. It offers world-class cultural
and family entertainment, featuring 26 international pavilions and millions
of visitors every season. As the most recent attraction within
Dubai Holding Entertainment, Dubai Parks and Resorts™ is the Middle East’s
largest multi-themed leisure and entertainment destination
comprising of three separate theme parks: MOTIONGATE™ Dubai,
LEGOLAND® Dubai and LEGOLAND® Water Park.

Dubai Holding Entertainment also operates Wild Wadi Waterpark,
open since 1999, becoming the first themed waterpark in the region.
It has received international awards
for the quality of its 30 rides, its safety record
and also for its innovation.

Leading destinations and attractions by Dubai Holding
Entertainment also include Coca-Cola Arena, the largest multipurpose indoor
arena in the Middle East; Roxy Cinemas,
a premiere theatre experience; and The Green Planet in City Walk,
a destination that recreates a tropical forest and many more.
